Global Premium Eggs Backed by International Evidence

Suseong's strategy for industrializing functional eggs and entering global markets through On-EIO (Bio Sulfur) technology

The global egg market was worth about $158.8 billion as of 2025, with Asia-Pacific accounting for about 58.8% of the total and showing the most dynamic growth. Through its unique On-EIO (Bio-Sulfur) technology, Suseong Agricultural Corporation goes beyond the commodity-egg market to offer functional premium eggs with non-detected antibiotics, high freshness, and high nutrition — presenting a "K-Sulfur Premium Meat" vision that can compete with global premium brands such as Vital Farms (USA) and Waitrose Duchy Organic (Europe).

On-EIO is an advanced bio-material made by completely removing the toxicity of naturally sourced mineral sulfur (detoxification) through a proprietary high-heat treatment and special microbial fermentation process, converting it into a nano-particle form with high bioavailability. Sulfur has powerful natural detoxifying, immune-boosting, and protein-synthesis effects, but its use has been limited by toxicity (arsenic, lead, etc.). Our processing technology removes this toxicity 100%, completing a "natural antibiotic" alternative that can be safely fed to livestock.

7. Stronger Immune System and Food Safety (Antibiotic-Free Evidence)

On-EIO itself has powerful natural antibacterial and detoxifying action. Feeding layers On-EIO feed has been academically proven to raise in-body glutathione levels — suppressing lipid oxidation and increasing baseline immune antibodies (IgA, IgM). For farms, this creates an environment where antibiotic use can be dramatically reduced or eliminated entirely.

In practice, composition analysis of On-EIO eggs confirmed non-detected results across roughly 70 antibiotics and residual chemical substances. This is a core indicator that meets the strict listing standards of global premium retail channels (Whole Foods Market, department-store food halls, etc.) that demand not just "antibiotic-free" but "scientifically verified safety." This enhanced immunity also raises resilience against diseases such as avian influenza (AI), functioning as a form of "insurance" for farm management that lowers the risk of mass culling.

Thanks to its nano-particle, hydrophilic properties, On-EIO disperses perfectly in the liquid environment of the digestive tract, increasing contact area with microbes by about 56x versus ordinary MSM, and raising methionine/cysteine synthesis efficiency by over 28.5% — making the egg's protein structure denser. The result is a yolk consumers experience as firmer and richer-flavored.
